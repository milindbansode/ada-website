Who We Are

Our Vision

To build a compassionate, inclusive, and equitable society where every individual—regardless of background—has access to opportunities, resources, and support to live with dignity, contribute meaningfully, and achieve their full potential.

Ambedkarite Dhamma Association (ADA) is dedicated to serving society through humanitarian, educational, and charitable initiatives. We support student development, food and book donation drives, community upliftment, and volunteer-driven service to create lasting, positive impact.

Our Purpose

Mission Statement

The mission of Ambedkarite Dhamma Association (ADA) is to improve the quality of life for individuals and communities through charitable, educational, and community-based initiatives that advance equality, inclusion, and social responsibility.

🤝

Community Upliftment

Designing and supporting programs that assist underserved and marginalized populations through access to essential resources, education, and community support.

🎓

Student Support & Education

Providing mentorship, guidance, and access to educational resources, including book donation drives and partnerships with student organizations, to promote academic success and career readiness.

🍲

Hunger Relief & Basic Needs

Organizing food donation drives and community support programs to address food insecurity and assist individuals and families in need.

📣

Community Awareness & Development

Promoting awareness of social issues and encouraging community-driven solutions that foster inclusion, equal opportunity, and responsible citizenship.

💚

Volunteerism & Civic Engagement

Encouraging individuals to actively participate in community service, fostering a culture of giving, leadership, and social responsibility.

🧓

Support for Seniors & Care Facilities

Engaging with nursing homes and senior care centers through outreach programs, companionship initiatives, and support services to improve well-being and quality of life.

☮️

Peace, Inclusion & Social Harmony

Promoting mutual respect, inclusivity, and constructive community engagement to strengthen social cohesion and community well-being.

What We Do

Our Programs

Book & Food Donation Drives

Regular collection and distribution of books and food to students and families facing economic hardship, removing barriers to education and basic well-being.

Student Mentorship

Partnerships with local student organizations, including the Charlotte Student Association, to provide mentorship, resources, and career guidance.

Hunger Relief Initiatives

Community food drives and hunger relief programs designed to support families in need within the local area.

Nursing Home & Senior Outreach

Visits, companionship, and support programs for elderly community members in nursing homes and care facilities.

Community Service & Volunteering

Organized volunteer opportunities that build a culture of giving, leadership, and civic responsibility across generations.

Awareness & Harmony Campaigns

Events and outreach that promote equality, inclusion, and peaceful, respectful community engagement.

Why It Matters

Our Impact

Every drive, mentorship hour, and outreach visit contributes to a more equitable and dignified life for the people we serve. As a young, growing organization, our impact is measured not only in numbers, but in the trust we build with the communities we serve.

"To build a compassionate and inclusive society rooted in the principles of equality, fraternity, wisdom, and peace — where every individual has the opportunity to grow, contribute, and live with dignity."
